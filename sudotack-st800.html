SUDOTACK ST-800 Review & Specifications

$45.99

Overview: The SUDOTACK ST-800 goes against the grain by including a full scissor arm stand (boom arm) right in the box. This provides a 'studio' setup immediately, getting the microphone off your desk and close to your mouth.

Key Features & Technical Specs

  • 192kHz/24Bit high sampling rate chipset
  • Full boom arm suspension kit
  • Double-layered pop filter and foam windscreen
  • Large 16mm diaphragm condenser

Pros

  • Incredible value for a full studio bundle
  • High resolution audio recording clarity
  • Boom arm prevents desk vibration pickup completely

Cons

  • Boom arm springs can be slightly squeaky during adjustment
  • Larger overall footprint than a desk stand

Should you actually buy it?

If you are aspiring podcasters and voiceover artists who want a true studio-arm setup under fifty bucks., I think the SUDOTACK ST-800 is a solid grab for your setup. I would definitely use this daily.
