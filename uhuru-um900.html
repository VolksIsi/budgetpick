Uhuru UM900 Kit Review & Specifications

$48.99

Overview: Another fantastic 'studio in a box', the Uhuru UM900 pairs a sleek, professional-looking condenser mic with a comprehensive array of mounting hardware, ensuring you have everything you need on day one.

Key Features & Technical Specs

  • 192kHz/24Bit audio chipset
  • Articulating suspension boom arm
  • Foam windscreen and mesh pop filter
  • Shock mount base

Pros

  • Comprehensive all-in-one value proposition
  • Very professional 'radio station' aesthetic
  • Crisp audio that responds well to EQ processing

Cons

  • Instructions for boom arm assembly can be confusing
  • Mic body picks up handling noise easily if touched

Should you actually buy it?

If you are users who want the entire professional podcasting look and capability delivered in a single purchase., I think the Uhuru UM900 Kit is a solid grab for your setup. I would definitely use this daily.
