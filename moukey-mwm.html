Moukey MCM-1 Review & Specifications

$19.99

Overview: While mostly a camera-mounted shotgun mic, the Moukey MCM-1 provides a hyper-directional option for desktop users wanting to mount it onto an arm and keep it completely out of camera frame while rejecting room noise.

Key Features & Technical Specs

  • Electret condenser shotgun capsule
  • Shock mount with cold-shoe and 1/4\" threading
  • Deadcat windmuff and foam cover included
  • No battery required (plug-in power)

Pros

  • Exceptional rejection of sounds from the sides and rear
  • Can be kept completely out of shot
  • Also works flawlessly with DSLR/Mirrorless cameras

Cons

  • Requires adapting to USB via a soundcard or aux-to-USB adapter
  • Not a dedicated desktop USB mic

Who is this for?

The Moukey MCM-1 is specifically aimed at YouTubers and videographers who want a mic that works on both their camera and their desktop for under $20.
