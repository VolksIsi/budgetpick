Koss M1 Broadcast Review & Specifications

$35.00

Overview: From the legendary audio company Koss, the M1 brings heritage audio tuning to the budget desk format. It focuses heavily on reproducing a completely natural, uncolored vocal tone without artificial bass boosting.

Key Features & Technical Specs

  • Flat-response condenser capsule
  • Zero-latency 3.5mm headphone jack
  • Acoustically transparent metal mesh grille

Pros

  • Incredibly accurate and natural vocal reproduction
  • Backed by a well-established audio brand legacy
  • Sturdy, no-nonsense construction

Cons

  • Requires proper post-processing to sound 'radio ready'

Should you actually buy it?
