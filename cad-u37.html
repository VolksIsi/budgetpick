CAD U37 USB Review & Specifications

$40.00

Overview: A staple in the entry-level audio scene, the CAD U37 is visually striking with its large profile. It uniquely features built-in hardware switches for pad reduction and bass roll-off, features usually reserved for luxury mics.

Key Features & Technical Specs

  • 10dB overload-protection switch (pad)
  • Hardware bass roll-off switch
  • Large condenser element
  • Included desktop stand and 10-foot USB cable

Pros

  • Hardware switches make adapting to loud sources easy
  • Bass roll-off eliminates low rumble and hums perfectly
  • Extra-long cable gives routing freedom

Cons

  • Design looks a bit dated compared to modern alternatives
  • Included tripod is prone to tipping due to the mic's sheer size

Should you actually buy it?

If you are recording vocalists or podcasters handling dynamic volume changes who need hardware attenuation., I think the CAD U37 USB is a solid grab for your setup. I would definitely use this daily.
