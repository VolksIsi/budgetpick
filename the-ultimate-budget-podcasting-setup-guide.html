The Ultimate Budget Podcasting Setup Guide

Starting a podcast shouldn't be an expensive endeavor. If you have an idea and a voice, you just need a few basic tools to get your show on Spotify and Apple Podcasts.

Step 1: The Microphone ($30-$50)

Do not use your laptop mic. Pick up a solid, multi-directional USB microphone. If you are recording with another person in the same room, grab the JLab Talk GO because it features an omnidirectional mode to capture you both.

Step 2: A Boom Arm ($15-$25)

Skip the cheap desk stand that comes with most mics. It will pick up every time you bump your table. Buy a cheap scissor boom arm on Amazon to get the mic suspended in the air close to your face. Alternatively, buy a bundle like the Fifine T669 Studio which includes the arm!

Step 3: Recording Software (Free)

Download Audacity. It is 100% free, open-source, and has been the industry standard for independent podcasters for two decades. It allows you to record multiple tracks, cut out mistakes, and export your final MP3 seamlessly.
