Behringer C-1U Review & Specifications

$45.00

Overview: The Behringer C-1U digitizes the critically acclaimed C-1 analog studio mic. It uses a relatively large diaphragm, making it one of the better budget choices for recording acoustic instruments alongside vocals.

Key Features & Technical Specs

  • Large 16mm diaphragm condenser
  • Die-cast metal body
  • Swivel stand mount included
  • Exceptionally flat frequency response

Pros

  • Excellent for recording acoustic guitars and vocals alike
  • Heavy, bomb-proof physical construction
  • Incredible dynamic range for a USB microphone

Cons

  • Does not include a desktop tripod stand out of the box
  • Gain levels can be quiet without software boosting

Should you actually buy it?

If you are budget-conscious acoustic musicians and home-studio singers who need a large diaphragm., I think the Behringer C-1U is a solid grab for your setup. I would definitely use this daily.
