Samson Go Mic Portable Review & Specifications

$39.00

Overview: The Samson Go Mic is the ultimate travel companion. It folds up to the size of a small wallet and brilliantly includes a built-in clip that attaches directly to your laptop screen or sits on your desk.

Key Features & Technical Specs

  • Extremely compact folding design
  • Custom laptop-screen mounting clip
  • Switchable Cardioid and Omnidirectional patterns
  • Protective zipper carrying case included

Pros

  • Unrivaled portability for content creation on the road
  • Clip design saves crucial workspace on cramped desks
  • Surprisingly full sound despite the tiny form factor

Cons

  • Micro-USB connection is outdated
  • Can pick up laptop fan noise closely since it attached to the screen

Should you actually buy it?

If you are digital nomads, traveling professionals, and vloggers requiring ultimate portability without audio compromise., I think the Samson Go Mic Portable is a solid grab for your setup. I would definitely use this daily.
